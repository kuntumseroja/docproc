PRIVACY POLICY
QuickPay Indonesia — Mobile Payment Application

Effective: January 2025

--------------------------------------------------------

About Us

QuickPay is a mobile payment application operated by PT QuickPay
Teknologi Nusantara. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Our office is located in Jakarta, Indonesia.

--------------------------------------------------------

What Information We Collect

When you use QuickPay, we collect:
- Your name and phone number
- Email address
- National ID (KTP) for verification
- Transaction history
- Device information
- Location data
- Biometric data (face ID for login)

--------------------------------------------------------

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide the QuickPay service, process transactions,
prevent fraud, comply with regulations, and send you marketing
communications about our products.

--------------------------------------------------------

Data Sharing

We may share your data with our business partners, payment processors,
affiliated companies, marketing partners, and when required by law.

Some of our service providers are located outside Indonesia
(in Singapore and the United States).

--------------------------------------------------------

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our
services. Transaction records are kept indefinitely for regulatory
reasons.

--------------------------------------------------------

Your Choices

You can update your profile information in the QuickPay app.
You can unsubscribe from marketing emails.

--------------------------------------------------------

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

--------------------------------------------------------

Contact Us

For questions about this policy, contact us at support@quickpay.id.

--------------------------------------------------------

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the
app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
